Stock Barcode For Odoo Community

This App will add the Barcode scanning functionality to Odoo community for version V11 to V17



  Looking for Demo or Support?

Features

 Scanning location Barcode 

 Scanning Operation Barcode to Open transfers of that Operation. 

 Scanning Transfer barcode such as Receipt, Delivery Order. 

 Scanning Product Barcode to Add new product or adding done quantity. 

Demo Video

Scanning Location Barcode

When users scan the barcode of stock location. It will open that location so users can easily navigate, edit, or view that stock location.

Scanning Delivery Operation Barcode

By scanning the barcode of the delivery order operation. The user will be redirected to the kanban view of delivery order operation. Where all the ready state records will be listed by default. in that user can click and open the record.

Scanning Receipt Operation Barcode

By scanning the barcode of the receipt or incoming operation. The user will be redirected to the kanban view of receipt operation. Where all the ready state records will be listed by default. in that user can click and open the record.

Scanning Ready Transfer Barcode

By scanning the barcode of the ready state, incoming shipment or delivery order. The User will get a detailed view of the record. Where data such as partner name, product name, reserved quantity, done quantity, enter manual barcode button, and validate button can be seen.

Scanning Done Transfer Barcode

By scanning the barcode of the done state, incoming shipment or delivery order. The user will get a detailed view of the record in readonly or view only mode. Where data such as partner name, product name, reserved quantity, done quantity can be seen only.

Scanning Draft Transfer Barcode

By scanning the barcode of the Draft state, incoming shipment or delivery order. The user will get a detailed view of the record in readonly or view only mode with no data until they mark such order as TODO.

Scanning Product Barcode to Add Product or Qty

By scanning the product, if the product is already there in system will find for such a line that consists of that product and will increase its done quantity. If system does not find any line which consists of that product. It will create a line and add it to the record. Whenever a quantity order product is added system will highlight that line by blinking it.
